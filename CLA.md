## How to sign

When you open your first pull request, the CLA Assistant bot will post a comment. Simply reply with the following to indicate agreement:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA.

This is a one-time process. Your signature will be recorded and all future contributions will be automatically approved.

## Why is this required?

MathFlow uses an open-core model: the Community Edition is open source under AGPL v3, while Pro and Team editions include additional features under commercial licenses. The CLA ensures we can continue to offer both open-source and commercial versions while incorporating community contributions.
