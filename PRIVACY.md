# MathFlow Privacy & Data Policy

## Self-Hosted (Community Edition)

When you self-host MathFlow:

- **All data stays on your server** — MathFlow has no telemetry, analytics, or phone-home functionality
- **AI features use YOUR API keys (BYOK)** — requests go directly from your server to OpenAI/Anthropic/Google. MathFlow never sees your keys or data
- **No tracking** — no cookies for analytics, no third-party scripts, no data collection whatsoever
- **You control everything** — your database, your backups, your infrastructure

## Hosted Version (mathflow.studio)

If you use the managed hosted version at mathflow.studio:

- Notes stored in encrypted PostgreSQL
- AI requests proxied through our server (we do not log prompts or responses)
- See full privacy policy at mathflow.studio/privacy

## Data Sovereignty

MathFlow CE is designed for users who need full control over their data. This is particularly important for:

- Academic researchers handling sensitive research data
- Institutions with data residency requirements
- Users in jurisdictions with strict data protection laws (GDPR, etc.)

Self-hosting MathFlow ensures your notes never leave your infrastructure.
